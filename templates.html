Upwork Proposal

Best for: Freelance marketplaces (Upwork, Fiverr, Freelancer.com)
🔷 Framework: Problem-Solution
Subject / Title
I can help you [solve specific problem from the job]
Opening Hook
Hi [Client Name], I read through your project and I see exactly what you're looking for. You need [core need] — and I've done this [X] times before.
The Problem You Solve
Most freelancers will tell you what they *can* do. I'll tell you what you *need* done. Your project needs someone who understands [key requirement], and I noticed the biggest challenge here is [specific challenge].
Your Solution
Here's my approach: [2-3 sentence breakdown of your solution]. I've delivered similar results for [similar client/industry], and I'm confident I can do the same for you.
Relevant Experience
Some quick context: I've been doing [your skill] for [X years] and have completed [X] similar projects. My most recent one was [brief 1-liner].
Call to Action
I'm available to start as early as [date]. Would you be open to a brief call to discuss your project in more detail?

Cold Email Proposal

Best for: Direct outreach to potential clients via email
🔷 Framework: Before-After
Subject Line
Quick idea for [Company Name]
Opening
Hi [Name], I've been following [Company Name] and noticed [specific observation / compliment]. Really impressive work with [specific initiative].
The Before (Current State)
Right now, I imagine you're spending a lot of time [current pain]. Most companies in your space struggle with [specific challenge], and it's costing them [lost opportunity / time / money].
The After (Transformation)
What if you could [desired outcome] without the [friction]? I helped [similar client] achieve [specific result] by [your approach].
Low-Friction CTA
Would you be open to a 10-minute call this week? No pitch — just a conversation about whether this makes sense for [Company Name].

Agency Partnership Proposal

Best for: B2B services, marketing agencies, consultancies
🔷 Framework: Credibility
Subject
Proposal: [Service] for [Company Name]
Executive Summary
[Company Name] needs [core outcome]. With [X years] of experience delivering [your specialty] for companies like [client references], I'm confident I can help you achieve [specific KPI].
Our Track Record
Over the past [X months], we've helped [X] clients achieve an average of [X% improvement]. Highlights include: [3 bullet points of results].
Proposed Approach
Phase 1: [Discovery & Audit]
Phase 2: [Strategy & Execution]
Phase 3: [Optimization & Scale]
Investment
[Price] for the initial [X weeks/months]. Includes [deliverables summary].
Next Steps
I'd love to schedule a strategy call to tailor this proposal to your specific needs. Available [times/dates].

Retainer Proposal

Best for: Ongoing monthly engagements, recurring services
🔷 Framework: Case Study
Subject
Retainer Proposal: Ongoing [Service] for [Company Name]
The Opportunity
Based on our conversation, I see a clear opportunity for us to work together on an ongoing basis. [Company Name] needs consistent [service], and a retainer model ensures you get priority access and predictable results.
Case Study (Similar Client)
When I started working with [Similar Company], they were [initial situation]. Within [X months] of our retainer engagement, they achieved [specific result]. The key was consistent [your approach].
Retainer Structure
[X] hours per month
Dedicated [service] support
Bi-weekly strategy calls
Priority turnaround ([X hours])
Monthly performance report
Monthly Investment
[$X/month] — [discount vs hourly] compared to project-based pricing.

Partnership Proposal

Best for: Joint ventures, cross-promotions, referral partnerships
🔷 Framework: Direct Pitch
Subject
Partnership idea: [Company A] x [Your Company]
The Idea
I see a natural fit between [Company Name] and what I do. You serve [your audience] with [their product], and my service [your offering] would be a perfect complement for your audience.
What I'm Proposing
A [revenue-share / cross-promotion / referral] partnership where:

• You [what they do — e.g., recommend my service]
• I [what you do — e.g., provide value to their audience]
• We split [terms — e.g., 30/70 on referred clients]
Why It Works
Your audience already trusts you. When you recommend my [service], they're far more likely to convert. Meanwhile, I deliver [value to their audience] — which makes you look even better to them.
First Step
Let's try a 30-day test. If it works for both of us, we make it permanent. Want to hop on a quick call to map out the details?
