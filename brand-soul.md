# Propello — Brand Soul

## Core Mission

**To turn every freelancer into a closer.**

Propello exists because the best proposal doesn't always win — the best *presented* proposal does. We're here to level that playing field. Not by selling a course. Not by selling hype. By shipping a tool that makes your proposals look, sound, and feel like they came from a $5M agency.

## Why "Propello"

Latin *propellere* — to drive forward, to push ahead.

That's what we do. Propello propels your proposals past the finish line. Past doubt. Past "I'll think about it." Past the 47 other proposals sitting in that inbox.

## Ethos (What We Stand *For*)

- **The $37 price isn't a discount — it's a statement.** Freelancers shouldn't pay SaaS rent forever. One purchase, lifetime value.
- **Great proposals aren't about writing more. They're about writing smarter.** The AI does the heavy lifting; you bring the soul.
- **Freelancing isn't a side hustle — it's a career.** We treat it with the seriousness it deserves.
- **Design matters.** A sloppy proposal signals a sloppy delivery. Propello makes you look buttoned-up before you walk in the door.
