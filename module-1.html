Getting Started with Propello

Set up your profile, learn the interface, and send your first AI-powered proposal.

📖 5 sections ⏱ 15 minutes

1. Welcome to Propello

Propello is an AI-powered proposal builder designed specifically for freelancers. Unlike generic AI writing tools, Propello learns from YOUR proposals, adapts to YOUR voice, and uses proven frameworks that actually convert.

By the end of this module, you'll have sent your first AI-generated proposal and set up your account for long-term success.

💡 Pro tip: The more proposals you write with Propello, the smarter it gets. It learns which frameworks, hooks, and language patterns work best for you.

2. Setting Up Your Profile

Your profile tells Propello who you are, what you do, and how you write. This is the foundation for every proposal the AI generates.

What to fill in:

  • Your Services — What do you offer? Web dev, design, copywriting, marketing?
  • Experience Level — Entry, intermediate, expert. This shapes the confidence in your proposals.
  • Writing Style — Professional, casual, direct. The AI matches your natural voice.
  • Portfolio Links — Add links to your best work. Propello can reference them in proposals.
  • Rate Range — Sets the pricing language the AI uses in your proposals.

Take 5 minutes to fill this out thoroughly. The quality of your output directly reflects the quality of your input.

3. Your First Proposal

Let's create your first proposal. Follow these steps:

Step 1: Find a job description

Grab any job post from Upwork, Fiverr, LinkedIn, or a cold outreach target. Copy the full description.

Step 2: Paste into Propello

Click "New Proposal" and paste the job description. Propello's AI will analyze it immediately.

Step 3: Choose a framework

Propello will suggest the best framework based on the job type. You can override:

  • Problem-Solution — Best for when the client has clearly stated a problem
  • Before-After — Best for transformation-focused roles
  • Credibility — Best when they need to trust your expertise
  • Direct Pitch — Best for short, high-volume applications
  • Case Study — Best for high-ticket or retainer clients

Step 4: Review and personalize

Read through the proposal. Add one or two personal sentences. Make it sound like you.

Step 5: Send

Copy, paste, and send. Track the response in your analytics.

⚡ Speed tip: Use Speed Mode when you're applying to multiple jobs. Generate 5 proposals in under 30 minutes.

4. Understanding Your Dashboard

Your dashboard is your command center. Here's what each section does:

  • Proposals Sent — Total proposals generated and sent through Propello
  • Response Rate — Percentage of proposals that got a reply
  • Frameworks Breakdown — Which frameworks are converting best for you
  • Win Analytics — Deeper insights into what's working and what's not
  • AI Content Coach — Personalized improvement suggestions based on your history

Check your dashboard weekly to spot trends and optimize your approach.

5. Next Steps

You're now ready to start winning more clients with Propello. Here's your action plan:

  • Complete your profile (5 min)
  • Send your first 3 proposals this week
  • Check your analytics after 7 days
  • Move on to Module 2: Mastering the 5 Frameworks

🎯 Your goal: Send 10 proposals in your first week. Track your response rate. Adjust your framework choice based on what works.
